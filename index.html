About Us

Long Walk Games was founded in May 2025 by Siri and Sean, two longtime friends and collaborators based in Galway, Ireland. The name is inspired by Galway’s iconic Long Walk — a nod both to the place we've made our home, and the journey we've taken (and still have ahead) to grow into the game developers we aspire to be.

We met while working on a small team at a big tech company, where conversations about books and games were a daily escape. Over time, our casual daydreams of "making a game someday" turned into something more serious. Even after parting ways professionally, we kept in touch — and eventually, over a pint on a rare sunny day in Galway, we realized we were both independently building up our game dev skills. That chat turned into a plan.

Our first project is called Book Nook. Book Nook is a cosy management and life sim where you nurture a forgotten public library through daily care and upgrades, quietly learning about the patrons through the books they check out and the items they leave behind.

We don’t have a motto, but we believe in getting better one day at a time. We aim to measure our progress not by comparing ourselves to others, but by looking back at who we were last week.
