Long Walk Games

About us

Long Walk Games was founded in May 2025 by Siri and Sean, two longtime friends and collaborators based in Galway, Ireland. The name is inspired by the iconic seafront area in Galway, and speaks to the journey we've taken (and still have ahead) to grow into the game developers we aspire to be.

Book Nook

Our first project is called Book Nook. Book Nook is a cosy management and life sim where you nurture a forgotten public library through daily care and upgrades, quietly learning about the patrons through the books they check out and the items they leave behind. We're working towards having our first playable prototype by November 2025.
